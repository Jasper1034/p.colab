Helpful Information for New Students

As a new student at the University of Wolverhampton, it's important to plan for daily expenses like groceries, transportation, and other living costs. Here are some tips to help you manage:

1. Getting Groceries

There are a variety of supermarkets in and around Wolverhampton where you can buy affordable groceries. Some popular options include:

  • Asda – A large store with a wide variety of food and household items. Located at Wolverhampton, WV10 9QY [View on Google Maps]
  • Tesco – Another large supermarket chain. They also offer delivery services. Located at Wolverhampton, WV1 1AD [View on Google Maps]
  • ALDI – Known for low-cost groceries, ALDI offers good quality at great prices. Located at Wolverhampton, WV1 1LT [View on Google Maps]
  • Co-op – A smaller store for quick and convenient shopping, often located close to residential areas. [View on Google Maps]

2. Public Transportation (Buses)

Wolverhampton has an efficient bus network for students to use. Some tips:

  • National Express West Midlands – The primary bus provider in the area. You can get a student discount on travel cards. [View Bus Station on Google Maps]
  • Travel Pass – You can buy a student bus pass for unlimited travel within Wolverhampton, or use contactless payment options on buses.
  • Route Information – The main bus station is located near Wolverhampton train station. Check local routes for the best travel options to and from campus.

3. Additional Local Services

Students also benefit from other local services and discounts:

  • Student Discounts – Many local businesses offer discounts to students. Show your student ID to save on food, shopping, and more.
  • Health Services – The University of Wolverhampton provides access to health services, including mental health support, so be sure to check out the resources available to you.
